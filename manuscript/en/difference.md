# Difference

-----

## Ch.1

-----

His toothbrush was on the sink.

I looked at it for a moment and left the apartment. I took nothing. There was no need to take anything. Whatever I needed could be had anywhere.

The room appeared in ten minutes. A room with a bed and a sink. There was nothing on the sink.

I could have ordered a toothbrush. If ordered, it would come within five minutes. I did not order it. I did not think about why.

There was nothing to do until evening. I ran a review.

-----

A different lover.

Possible. Say the conditions you want and you could meet one. Any conditions at all.

I can't tell the difference.

A different pleasure.

Possible. The kinds were not lacking. I can't tell the difference. It is possible. Being possible and wanting are separate matters.

An additional lover.

Possible. Two would do, three would do. I can't tell the difference. More does not mean better.

The list went on. They all ended much the same. I can't tell the difference — meaning either way was fine, and either way being fine meant there was no reason to decide.

I did not stop the review. There was no reason to stop either.

-----

Evening came. Food arrived. The taste was exact. It tasted the same wherever I ate. I had never once thought that strange.

Outside the window went dark.

I thought of the toothbrush. Five minutes if I ordered it. I had not been ordering it.

Is my lover running the same review right now.

There the review stopped.

The case where he reviews a different lover. The case where he reviews a different pleasure. The case where he concludes he can't tell the difference.

That case and the present case — there I could tell the difference.

It was the first time.

All evening I had only said I can't tell the difference, but this one thing I could tell. Among the things where either way was fine, one thing came up that was not.

I understood the reason I had not ordered, too.

I went back.

-----

## Ch.2

-----

The door opened.

He came in.

I said nothing. He said nothing either.

He went to wash. The water ran. The usual length.

On the sink was his toothbrush.

Mine too.

-----

*Difference — end*
